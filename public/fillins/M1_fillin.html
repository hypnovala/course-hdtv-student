The Masculine Attention Reset — 5 Daily Disciplines
  • Morning anchor: 10 minutes of silence before the phone. No exceptions.
  • Single-tasking: One task. Full attention. Nothing else open or running.
  • Phone boundaries: Three designated times to check. Not constantly, not compulsively.
  • Presence drill: Before entering any room, pause. Breathe. Choose your state.
  • Evening audit: Rate your mental command for the day: 1–10. Write it down.
